Bobby Mackey's Music World

Haunting History and Information

Are you looking for a haunting time? Do you want to experience the fun of a creepy, spooky nightclub? Well head over to Bobby Mackey's Music World in Wilder, Kentucky for a scary time. Bobby Mackey's Music World is a nightclub and honky tonk owned by country singer, Bobby Mackey. Legends say the nightclub is the site of murders, suicides and hauntings. It is the most haunted nightclub in America. The site of the Music World was originally a slaughterhouse in the 19th century. The only remains of the original structure is a well that was dug in the basement. This well was was used to gather the drained blood and refuse from the animals. The basement has been said to be a ritual site for occultists, the well was used to hide the remains of the animals they sacrificed.


Haunted Spirits

The site is said to be haunted by spirits including Pearl Bryan, whose corpse was found in a field 2.5 miles from the site. Legends say that her murderers were Satanists who cursed the location and vowed to haunt everyone involved in the case. Scott Jackson and Alonzo Walling threw her head in the infamous well.

Another spirit that haunts Bobby Mackey's music World is Johanna. Johanna was a pregnant dancer who committed suicide with poison, after her father murdered her loved one, Robert Randall, singer of the club. Her father hung him in the dressing-room. Stories say she attempted to kill her father with the poison, before killing herself. Her body was discovered later in the infamous basement. Johanna is still haunting the basement, awaiting for her love, her poem is still written on the wall of Bobby Mackey's.
