How to make Acai Bowls

Acai bowls look like ice cream, almost taste like ice cream, and make you feel good about your breakfast choice. It’s what breakfast dreams are made of.

Ingredients

  • Acai Purée (Sambazon)
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach
  • 1/2 small banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup milk

    • Toppings

  • 1/2 small banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of strawberries, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of granola
  • 1 tbsp shredded coconut
  • Drizzle of honey

    • Directions

  • Add to your blender: spinach, banana, acai purée, mango, and milk. Blend on low until large chunks are broken down. Gradually increase speed. Blend until all ingredients are mixed, and you have achieved desired consistency.
  • Pour the acai mix into a bowl, and gather your desired toppings.
  • Add your toppings in any order you desire, and drizzle honey (optional).

    • Prep time: 5 minutes, Blend time: 3 minutes

    Enjoy!
